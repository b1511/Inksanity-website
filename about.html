Where
It all started

The owner and creator of tattoo studio “Inksanity” is Sallam Ahmadi. Several years ago he started a small business in the barber industry, but deep in his heart it was not what he truly desired.

In time, he finds passion in drawing and that turns out to be the game changer for him. This passion of his leads to the life-changing decision - to open a tattoo studio in Northern Denmark.

the
studio

The studio itself has a great location right in the centre of Aalborg city. Situated on one of the main boulevards - Boulevarden and has its doors open from Monday to Friday.

The studio has been fully estblished by the owner. The interior is designed in a way to achieve cosy atmosphere, reflecting the style of the artist. Moreover, the hygiene is on a high level, thus the clients feel in safe hands.
