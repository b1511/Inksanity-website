Where
It all started

The owner and creator of tattoo studio “Inksanity” is Sallam Ahmadi. Several years ago he started a small business in the barber industry, but deep in his heart it was not what he truly desired.

In time, he finds passion in drawing and that turns out to be the game changer for him. This passion of his leads to the life-changing decision - to open a tattoo studio in Northern Denmark.
